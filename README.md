## General Resources

- [interviewing.io](https://interviewing.io) - Anonymous mock interviews with engineers from top companies.
- [Pramp](https://pramp.com) - Free peer-to-peer mock interview platform.
- [Blind](https://www.teamblind.com) - Anonymous community for tech professionals to discuss interviews and offers.
- [Levels.fyi](https://levels.fyi) - Compensation data and interview experiences across tech companies.

## Software Engineering

- [tech-interview-handbook](https://github.com/yangshun/tech-interview-handbook) - Curated interview preparation materials for busy engineers.
- [system-design-primer](https://github.com/donnemartin/system-design-primer) - Learn how to design large-scale systems.
- [coding-interview-university](https://github.com/jwasham/coding-interview-university) - A complete computer science study plan to become a software engineer.
- [LeetCode Patterns](https://github.com/seanprashad/leetcode-patterns) - Key patterns to solve LeetCode problems efficiently.
- [NeetCode](https://neetcode.io) - Curated roadmap of 150 LeetCode problems organized by pattern.
- 📖 [The 0→1 SWE Interview Playbook](https://amazon.com/dp/B0H1F83LCM?tag=sirjohnnymai-20) - Silicon Valley system for coding interviews, system design, behavioral rounds, and offer negotiation.
- [Software Engineer Interview Guide](https://sirjohnnymai.com/blog/software-engineer-interview-guide/) - Free comprehensive guide covering all SWE interview rounds.

## Data Science

- [data-science-interview-questions-and-answers](https://github.com/iamtodor/data-science-interview-questions-and-answers) - Curated DS interview questions and answers.
- [Data Science Cheatsheet](https://github.com/aaronwangy/Data-Science-Cheatsheet) - Quick-reference guide for DS concepts.
- [ML Interview Prep](https://github.com/khangich/machine-learning-interview) - Comprehensive ML interview resource.
- 📖 [The 0→1 Data Scientist Interview Playbook](https://amazon.com/dp/B0H1NWZB2R?tag=sirjohnnymai-20) - FAANG data science: SQL, statistics, product sense, A/B testing, ML system design.
- [Data Scientist Interview Guide](https://sirjohnnymai.com/blog/data-scientist-interview-guide/) - Free comprehensive guide for DS interviews.

## Product Management

- [open-product-management](https://github.com/LisaDziuba/open-product-management) - Curated open-source resources for product managers.
- [Product Management Exercises](https://www.productmanagementexercises.com) - Practice PM interview questions.
- [Cracking the PM Interview](https://www.lewis-lin.com/blog) - Lewis Lin's PM interview frameworks.
- 📖 [The 0→1 Product Manager Interview Playbook](https://amazon.com/dp/B0GWWJQ2S3?tag=sirjohnnymai-20) - Product sense, metrics, behavioral stories, strategy cases, and PM offers.
- [Product Manager Interview Guide](https://sirjohnnymai.com/blog/product-manager-interview-guide/) - Free comprehensive guide for PM interviews.

## AI/ML Engineering

- [Applied ML](https://github.com/eugeneyan/applied-ml) - Papers and tech blogs about ML applied in production.
- [ML Systems Design](https://github.com/chiphuyen/machine-learning-systems-design) - A booklet on ML systems design with exercises.
- [Awesome Production ML](https://github.com/EthicalML/awesome-production-machine-learning) - Curated list of ML production tools.
- 📖 [The 0→1 AI Engineer Interview Playbook](https://amazon.com/dp/B0H2CML9XD?tag=sirjohnnymai-20) - GenAI interviews at OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and Meta.

## Data Engineering

- [Awesome Data Engineering](https://github.com/igorbarinov/awesome-data-engineering) - Curated list of data engineering resources.
- [Data Engineering Zoomcamp](https://github.com/DataTalksClub/data-engineering-zoomcamp) - Free data engineering course with hands-on projects.
- 📖 [The 0→1 Data Engineer Interview Playbook](https://amazon.com/dp/B0H249WDPZ?tag=sirjohnnymai-20) - SQL, pipelines, data modeling, cloud — the complete system for DE interviews.

## Solutions Architect
